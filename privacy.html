Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This application ("Maplestory Content Tool") is a personal tool used solely by its developer to publish gameplay content to TikTok. It does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties.

Data We Access

The application accesses only the TikTok account explicitly authorized by the account owner during the OAuth login flow. No data is transmitted to any third-party server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.

Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on the developer's own machine and are never shared or uploaded.
